# Product Overview

MockupSuite is an AI-powered mockup generator that creates professional product photography and mockups using Google's Gemini AI.

## Core Features

- **Scene Generation Mode**: Transform product photos into studio-quality images with custom backgrounds and scenes
- **Product Mockup Mode**: Apply designs to product templates (apparel, home goods, print, tech)
- **Multi-language Support**: English, Turkish, Spanish
- **Project Management**: Organize mockups into projects with persistent storage
- **Brand Kit**: Apply watermarks and manage brand colors
- **Prompt Templates**: Save and reuse successful prompts
- **Gallery**: Browse, search, and manage generated mockups

## User Flow

1. Upload product photos or design files
2. Describe desired scene or select product template
3. Generate mockups using Gemini 2.5 Flash
4. Save, download, or reuse generated images
